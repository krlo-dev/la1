List of my favorites Singers

Nombre: michael Jackson

Biography: Michael Jackson, born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana, was an American singer, songwriter, and dancer known as the "King of Pop." He started his music career at a young age with his brothers in the Jackson 5, quickly becoming the group's lead singer. His solo career took off in the 1970s, with groundbreaking albums like Off the Wall (1979), Thriller (1982), and Bad (1987). Thriller remains the best-selling album of all time.

Nombre: the weeknd

Biography: The Weeknd, born Abel Makkonen Tesfaye on February 16, 1990, in Toronto, Canada, is a Grammy Award-winning singer, songwriter, and record producer. Known for his genre-blending music that fuses R&B, pop, and electronic elements, The Weeknd rose to fame in the early 2010s with his mixtapes House of Balloons, Thursday, and Echoes of Silence. These works garnered critical acclaim for their moody, atmospheric sound. His first major label album, Kiss Land (2013), was followed by commercial successes like Beauty Behind the Madness (2015), which included hits like "Can't Feel My Face" and "The Hills." He further solidified his status with albums such as Starboy (2016) and After Hours (2020), featuring the record-breaking single "Blinding Lights.".

Nombre: Drake

Biography: Drake, born Aubrey Drake Graham on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Canada, is a globally renowned rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor. He initially gained recognition as an actor on the Canadian teen drama Degrassi: The Next Generation. However, his music career took off with his early mixtapes, particularly So Far Gone (2009), which produced hit singles like "Best I Ever Had" and "Successful.
